A particle moves along a straight line with position function:

s(t) = 2t² − 4t + 3  (meters, t in seconds)

(a) Find the average velocity over [1, 3]
(b) Find the instantaneous velocity at t = 2s
Your Work
✅ Full Solution (a) Average Velocity [1, 3] — NO differentiation!
s(1) = 2(1)² − 4(1) + 3 = 2 − 4 + 3 = 1
s(3) = 2(3)² − 4(3) + 3 = 18 − 12 + 3 = 9
Avg vel = (9 − 1) / (3 − 1) = 8/2 = 4 m/s

(b) Instantaneous Velocity at t = 2 — DIFFERENTIATE!
v(t) = ds/dt = 4t − 4
v(2) = 4(2) − 4 = 8 − 4 = 4 m/s
